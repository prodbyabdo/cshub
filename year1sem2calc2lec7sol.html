AREAS BETWEEN CURVES PRACTICE - LECTURE 7

Areas Between Curves Practice

Problem 1
\[ \text{Area between }y=x^2\text{ and }y=x \]
Intersections: \(x^2=x\Rightarrow x=0,1\)
\(\int_0^1(x-x^2)dx\)
\[ = \frac{1}{6} \]
Problem 2
\[ \text{Area between }y=\sin x\text{ and }y=\cos x,\;0\le x\le\frac{\pi}{2} \]
Cross at \(x=\pi/4\)
\(\int_0^{\pi/4}(\cos x-\sin x)dx+\int_{\pi/4}^{\pi/2}(\sin x-\cos x)dx\)
\[ = 2(\sqrt{2}-1) \]
Problem 3
\[ \text{Area enclosed by }y=x^3-x \]
Zeros at \(-1,0,1\); area above on \([-1,0]\), below on \([0,1]\)
\(\int_{-1}^0(x^3-x)dx + \int_0^1(x-x^3)dx\)
\[ = \frac{1}{2} \]
Problem 4
\[ \text{Area between }y=e^x,\;y=e^{-x},\;x=0\text{ to }x=1 \]
\(e^x>e^{-x}\) on \([0,1]\)
\(\int_0^1(e^x-e^{-x})dx\)
\[ = e - e^{-1} - 2 \]
Problem 5
\[ \text{Area between }x=y^2\text{ and }x=y+2 \]
Integrate w.r.t. \(y\): intersections \(y=-1,2\)
\(\int_{-1}^{2}(y+2-y^2)dy\)
\[ = \frac{9}{2} \]
