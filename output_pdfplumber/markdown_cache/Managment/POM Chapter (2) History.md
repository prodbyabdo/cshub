> The Modern Era:
>  Management by Objective (MBO) is the process of
> defining specific objectives necessary to achieve the
> organization’s goals.

> organization’s objectives and defined their individual

**Category**: Raw Text

> Management theories
> 
> 1- Scientific Management Theory
>  Frederick Winslow Taylor developed and published his Scientific
> Management Theory in 1909.
>  Believes that it is vital to find the most effective way to complete each and
> every task, no matter how small.
>  Task should be completed as efficiently as possible and everyone should
> be assigned a particular job based on their skills and abilities and must be
> evaluated based on the quantity and quality of their work.
>  It was extremely important to monitor and train your employees on the
> tasks they are assigned to. By ensuring your employees are efficient at
> their work, the output will be larger and of a higher quality.
>  This theory had a huge impact on the way companies operated and how
> they were able to create a more balanced pay system, better training, and
> a more efficient workforce.

>  Max Weber created the bureaucratic management theory in 1921.

>  leaders are viewed in a negative light while managers are appreciated and
