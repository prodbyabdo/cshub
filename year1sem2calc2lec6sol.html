TRIG & CLEVER SUBSTITUTION PRACTICE - LECTURE 6

Trig Substitution & Clever Substitution Practice

Problem 1
\[ \int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{x^2+4}} \]
Let \(x=2\tan\theta\), \(dx=2\sec^2\theta\,d\theta\)
\(\sqrt{x^2+4}=2\sec\theta\); integral becomes \(\int\sec\theta\,d\theta\)
\[ = \ln\left|x+\sqrt{x^2+4}\right|+C \]
Problem 2
\[ \int \frac{\sqrt{9-x^2}}{x^2}\,dx \]
Let \(x=3\sin\theta\)
\(\int\frac{3\cos\theta}{9\sin^2\theta}\cdot 3\cos\theta\,d\theta = \int\cot^2\theta\,d\theta\)
\[ = -\frac{\sqrt{9-x^2}}{x}-\sin^{-1}\!\left(\frac{x}{3}\right)+C \]
Problem 3
\[ \int \frac{dx}{x^2\sqrt{x^2-1}} \]
Let \(x=\sec\theta\)
Integral reduces to \(\int\cos\theta\,d\theta\)
\[ = \frac{\sqrt{x^2-1}}{x}+C \]
Problem 4
\[ \int \frac{dx}{1+e^x} \]
Multiply top and bottom by \(e^{-x}\)
\(\int\frac{e^{-x}}{e^{-x}+1}dx\); let \(u=e^{-x}+1\)
\[ = x - \ln(1+e^x)+C \]
Problem 5
\[ \int \frac{dx}{1+\sin x} \]
Weierstrass: let \(t=\tan(x/2)\)
\(\sin x=\frac{2t}{1+t^2}\), \(dx=\frac{2}{1+t^2}dt\)
Integral becomes \(\int\frac{2}{(1+t)^2}dt\)
\[ = -\frac{2}{1+\tan(x/2)}+C \]
