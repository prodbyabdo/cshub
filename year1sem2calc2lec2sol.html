Substitution & Completing the Square Practice

Problem 1
\[ \int \frac{4x}{(x^2+3)^2}\,dx \]
Let \(u=x^2+3\), \(du=2x\,dx\)
\(\int \frac{2}{u^2}du = -\frac{2}{u}\)
\[ = -\frac{2}{x^2+3}+C \]
Problem 2
\[ \int e^{3x}\,dx \]
Let \(u=3x\), \(du=3\,dx\)
\(\frac{1}{3}\int e^u du\)
\[ = \frac{1}{3}e^{3x}+C \]
Problem 3
\[ \int \frac{\cos(\ln x)}{x}\,dx \]
Let \(u=\ln x\), \(du=\frac{1}{x}dx\)
\(\int \cos u\,du\)
\[ = \sin(\ln x)+C \]
Problem 4
\[ \int_0^1 x\sqrt{1-x^2}\,dx \]
Let \(u=1-x^2\), \(du=-2x\,dx\); limits: \(u(0)=1,u(1)=0\)
\(-\frac{1}{2}\int_1^0 u^{1/2}du = \frac{1}{2}\cdot\frac{2}{3}\)
\[ = \frac{1}{3} \]
Problem 5
\[ \int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{5-4x-x^2}} \]
Complete the square: \(5-4x-x^2=9-(x+2)^2\)
\(\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{9-(x+2)^2}}\)
\[ = \sin^{-1}\!\left(\frac{x+2}{3}\right)+C \]
