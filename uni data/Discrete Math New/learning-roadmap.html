💡Pro Tips

1. YouTube > University Lectures
For practical web dev, YouTube tutorials are often better than textbooks.
2. Build Projects, Don't Just Watch
Pause tutorials and code along. Then build your own version.
3. Read MDN Documentation
developer.mozilla.org is the official source of truth.
4. Join Communities
Discord servers, Reddit r/webdev, Twitter/X dev community.
5. Your University Doesn't Define You
Self-taught developers get hired at top companies every day. Your portfolio matters more than your degree.
